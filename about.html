Who We Are

Future Books is more than just an online bookstore; it's a digital ecosystem born from the legacy of Serangreen Holdings (PVT) Ltd. Founded with a vision to revolutionize the reading experience in Sri Lanka, we bridge the gap between timeless traditions and modern convenience.

Our journey began in the vibrant heart of Galle, fueled by a passion for preserving the rich heritage of Sinhala literature while making global English masterpieces accessible to everyone, everywhere.
